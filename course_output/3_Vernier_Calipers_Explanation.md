## Key Terminology
* **Vernier Calipers**: A precision measuring instrument used to measure distances, widths, and heights of objects.
* **Metric Vernier Calipers**: A type of Vernier calipers calibrated in metric units (e.g., millimeters).

## Practical Application
Vernier calipers are widely used in various fields, including:
* **Engineering**: For precise measurements of parts and components.
* **Science**: In laboratories for measuring samples and equipment.
* **Quality Control**: To ensure accurate dimensions of products.
