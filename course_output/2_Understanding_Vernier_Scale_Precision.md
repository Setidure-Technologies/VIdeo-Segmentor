## Objectives
- Understand the concept of Vernier scale precision.
- Learn about the metric Vernier calipers.
- Understand the precision of 1/50 mm.

## Notes
The Vernier scale is a precision measuring instrument used to measure small distances. 
- The metric Vernier calipers provide precise measurements.
- The precision is 1/50 mm, which equals 0.02 mm.

## Definitions
- **Vernier Calipers**: A precision measuring instrument used to measure small distances.
- **Metric Vernier Calipers**: Vernier calipers calibrated in metric units.
- **Precision**: The degree of accuracy of a measurement.

## Practical Application
- Used in engineering and manufacturing for precise measurements.
- Essential in quality control and inspection processes.
- Applied in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and construction.
